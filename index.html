About Us

Welcome to Tuskers Cricket Club, a proud member of the Saskatoon cricketing community. We are dedicated to promoting the sport of cricket in Saskatchewan, fostering a spirit of sportsmanship, and providing a platform for players of all skill levels to excel.

Club History

2012

Founded as "Huskies Cricket Club".

2015-2021

Operations paused due to unseen circumstances.

2022

Re-established under the leadership of Captain Saranga Bandara & President Amila Prasad.

2024

Officially renamed to "Tuskers Cricket Club". First season under new name led by Captain Andrew Fernando reaching the semi-finals.

2026

Current leadership: Captain Diluka Ranasinghe & President Suneru Perera.
